Flagship Project

Tonga CRVS & National ID Hub

Kingdom of Tonga — National Digital Identity & Civil Registration Platform
In collaboration with the Ministry of Justice, Kingdom of Tonga & the World Bank
⭐ FLAGSHIP

A national-scale digital transformation initiative for the Kingdom of Tonga, modernising the entire Civil Registration and Vital Statistics (CRVS) ecosystem and establishing a MOSIP-powered National Identity Hub. The platform enables the government to issue and manage national digital identities while automating vital event registrations — Birth, Marriage, and Death — across the country.

eSignet — National Authentication Layer
Google-style OAuth / OpenID Connect Authentication Platform

eSignet is MOSIP's national digital authentication module — similar in concept to "Sign in with Google" but for government identity. It enables citizens to securely authenticate using their national digital identity across all government services.

  • Customised the eSignet login flow to match Tonga government authentication requirements
  • Customised the eSignet UI to align with national branding and user experience standards
  • Implemented backend integration connecting eSignet with the MOSIP identity registry
  • Ensured secure token exchange and session management across government service portals
CRVS System Integration
